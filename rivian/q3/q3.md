Sessionization

The Scenario: Rivian gets a stream of coordinates. A "Trip" starts when the car moves and ends when it hasn't moved for 5 minutes.

The Data: A table gps_logs with vehicle_id, timestamp, and is_moving (boolean).

The Task: Calculate the total number of trips each vehicle took today.

The Strategy: This is a "Gaps and Islands" problem. You need to identify when a new trip starts by comparing a row to its predecessor.
